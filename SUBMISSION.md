## Description

> A small NTSC Atari 2600 demo with color-cycling raster bars, a large
> SQUEEPTY playfield title, a bouncing sprite, animated checkerboard, scrolling
> ticker, and a two-voice TIA melody. It runs as a fixed 262-scanline frame and
> needs no controls.
